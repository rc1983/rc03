Empire's Edge


This is my favorite map in Battlefield 1.

It supports a lot of different kinds of gameplay, and on paper there is room for naval combat as well, but that aspect of Battlefield 1 is so underwhelming, its not even worth mentioning in my opinion. Even the Turning Tides DLC which was supposed to heavily focus on naval combat was boring at best in that aspect. Disregarding that though, there is plenty to do for everyone else.

This is one of the most fun maps for long range sniping. Infantry has plenty to do between B, C, D, F capture points. Tanks can really shine on this map. It is especially fun taking out aircrafts with tanks. The behemoth on this map is the dreadnaught, and I found this particular behemoth to be the most boring. The gameplay devolves into slow and point and click adventure. Its only a step above the Commander mode in Battlefield 4.

The excellent gameplay, combined with the beautiful visuals make this one of the best Battlefield maps for me.

Grade: A
